Applefrittr's Chili

Description

Easy make recipe to Applefrittr's Bomb Ass Chili. Passed down from father to son, this dish is THE go too after a long day's work. Pairs great with crackers, rice, or fantastic on it's own! Spice it up with fresh onions and jalapenos as garnish.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef, chicken, or turkey - player's choice
  • cooking oil
  • half an onion
  • clove of garlic
  • canned pinto beans
  • canned navy beans
  • canned diced tomatos
  • caned tomato paste - small
  • package chili seasoning OR throw in whatever is in your spice cabinet that logically woould go into a savory, smokey, and spicy dish

Steps

  1. Chop and sautee the onion and the garlic in a small amount of oil. Use a big pot for best results and no mess
  2. Once onion and garlic carmelize, throw in ground meat. Thourghly cook meat until brown.
  3. Open up your canned beans, tomatos, and the small can of tomato paste. Not draining the liquids from the cans will make the chili more soup like, play with this to your liking.
  4. Stir ingredients together evenly to ensure you got a nice mix in your pot.
  5. Add your seasonings and mix!
  6. Cover and let simmer for 30 mins
  7. Serve in a bowl over rice!
  8. (Optional) Top with fresh diced onions and jalapenos! Sprinkle shredded cheese!
