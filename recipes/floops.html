Bowl of Loops!

Description

Sometimes you need a sugar overdose in the morning. No better way to do this than a bowl of Fruit Loops!

Ingredients

  • Fruit Loops - Kellogs (I'm am not a sponsor, I jsut love Fruit Loops)
  • Milk
  • Sugar addiction

Steps

  1. Fight the urge to go back to sleep and get yourself up and out of bed. Yes we all agree school/work sucks and sleep is better.
  2. While rubbing the sleep out of your eyes, stumble into your kitchen. Don't trip over your clothes you haphazardly tossed onto the floor before going to bed the night before.
  3. Reach into the kitchen cabnets and grab yourself a bowl and your trusty spoon.
  4. Grab the Fruit Loops you left on the table from the morning prior. Check to make sure they're not stale.
  5. Pour as much as you like into your bowl. If you use the last bit, set yourself a reminder to pick up more after work. If you're still in school, text your mom/dad to pick some more up after work.
  6. Add in your milk. I like my Fruit Loops crunchy throughout the experience so I use a little less than recommended. Play with this until you find YOUR sweet spot.
  7. Go to town!
