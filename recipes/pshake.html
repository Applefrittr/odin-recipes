Protein Shake!

Description

Tired of spending $15 on a protein shake after a workout? Look no further! Easy to make protein shake to help fuel those gains you've worked so hard to achieve at the Iron Temple. Wheymen.

Ingredients

  • Gold Standard Whey Protein - Chocolate (or whatever you're into)
  • Milk - Dairy, Almond, Oak, whatever (no breast milk please cause that's just weird)
  • Banana
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Grab your blender and fill it with a good amount of the milk of your choice
  2. Take a scoop of that chocloate gold - don't be shy of a little extra - and dump it into the blender with your milk
  3. Peel your banana and toss it in
  4. Grab a handful of ice and add. Add extra to make your smoothie thick
  5. BLEND!
  6. Pour out the contents into a cup or your favorite thermos and ENJOY!
